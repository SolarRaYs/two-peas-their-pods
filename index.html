Hi, I’m Maria

I am so excited to cook and bake with you. The recipes you’ll find here are simple, fresh, family friendly, and sometimes a little decadent. I love vegetables, but I also have a sweet tooth. I am a cookie connoisseur and believe cookies make the world a better place. The kitchen is my happy place and the center of our home. It isn’t always clean, but it is always filled with laughter, good conversation, dancing, and delicious food. I hope you enjoy our recipes!
